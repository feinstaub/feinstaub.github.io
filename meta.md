Why mdwiki?
-----------
http://dynalon.github.io/mdwiki

* CON:

  * bugs

      two entries instead of one (not reported yet)
* Others

    * CON: navigation links are on the top only
  * wordpress.com
    * CON: no automatic navigation generation possible
